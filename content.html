Course Descriptions

If you're learning to program for the first time, or if you're coming from a different language, this course will give you the basics for coding in JavaScript.

Next, you’ll explore the basics of the language, giving plenty of examples. Lastly, you’ll put your knowledge to work on a modern web page.

What you’ll learn
  • Recognize the importance of understanding your objectives
  • Identify the fundamentals of composing a successful close
  • Explore how to connect with your audience
  • Examine ways to connect with your audience
  • Break down the best ways to exude executive presence
  • Explore how to communicate the unknown
